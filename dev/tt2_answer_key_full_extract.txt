5. (3 marks) As far as thermodynamics is concerned define a closed system. Write a sentence! Next give 
an example of a closed system. 
A closed system is any system in which energy can be transferred between the system and its 
surroundings but matter cannot. 
Example: a can of beer, the cylinder of compressed air in the lab (when not in use, valve closed)
